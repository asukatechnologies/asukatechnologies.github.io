UpRange Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

UpRange (the “App”) is operated by Asuka Technologies LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”), located at 175 Kinderkamack Road #1041, Park Ridge, NJ 07656. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and your choices. Questions? Contact us at contact@asukatechnologies.com.

By creating an account or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the App.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide:

  • Account information: email address, and a password or one-time verification code (OTP) used to sign in and reset access.
  • Profile information: display name, username, and an optional profile photo (avatar).
  • Content you create: shooting sessions, stages, scores, notes, and comments.
  • Media you upload: videos and images you attach to sessions or posts.
  • Communications: messages you send us (e.g., support requests, feedback).

Information created through your use of the App:

  • Activity and performance data you record (e.g., session times, scores, stage configurations).
  • Data from connected Bluetooth (BLE) shot-timer devices you choose to pair, such as shot timestamps and timer readings. This data is transmitted from the device to your phone via Bluetooth; we do not access your device’s other Bluetooth connections.
  • Bookmarks and saved posts.

Information collected automatically:

  • Basic technical and diagnostic information such as device type, operating system version, app version, and crash or error logs.

We do not knowingly collect precise GPS location, and we do not sell your personal information.

2. How we use information

  • Create and manage your account and authenticate you (including OTP sign-in and password reset);
  • Provide core features: recording sessions, syncing stages, publishing and viewing posts, saving and bookmarking content;
  • Store and display your content and media;
  • Operate offline mode and sync your data when you reconnect;
  • Maintain, secure, debug, and improve the App;
  • Communicate with you about the App, including service and support messages; and
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

3. How your content is shared

  • Public posts and profiles: If you publish a post or make profile content public, that content (including your username, avatar, and any attached video/images) is visible to other users in the App’s Discover, Gallery, and similar features. Do not publish anything you want to keep private.
  • Private content: Sessions, stages, and saved items you do not publish remain associated with your account and are not shown publicly, subject to our access for operating and supporting the App.
  • We do not sell your personal information or share it for third-party advertising.

4. Service providers (sub-processors)

We use trusted third parties to run the App. They process data only to provide services to us:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and file storage (including profile avatars).
  • Cloudflare (including R2 storage) — storage and delivery of uploaded media such as videos and images.
  • Apple App Store and/or Google Play — app delivery and crash reporting.

5. Data storage, location, and retention

  • Local storage: Much of your data is stored locally on your device (including offline-mode data) and remains until you delete it or uninstall the App.
  • Cloud storage: Content you upload or publish is stored with our service providers (Supabase, Cloudflare R2) and retained while your account is active or as needed to provide the App.
  • We retain information as long as necessary for the purposes above and to meet legal obligations, then delete or de-identify it.

6. Your choices and rights

  • Access/update: You can view and edit profile information in the App.
  • Delete content: You can delete sessions, stages, and posts you created.
  • Delete account: You can request deletion of your account and associated data in the App’s settings or by emailing contact@asukatechnologies.com. We will delete or de-identify your data except where we must retain it by law.
  • Depending on where you live (e.g., California/CCPA, EU/UK/GDPR), you may have additional rights such as access, correction, deletion, and portability, and the right to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these, contact contact@asukatechnologies.com. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including authentication and access controls provided by our infrastructure. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect their information. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact contact@asukatechnologies.com and we will delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version and change the “Last updated” date, and, where required, provide additional notice.
