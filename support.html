Getting help

If you have a question, run into a problem, or want to report a bug in any Asuka Technologies product, email us at contact@asukatechnologies.com. Please include the app name, your device model, and the operating system version so we can help you faster.

Response time

We aim to respond to all support requests within two business days.

Open-source projects

For our open-source software, please open an issue on the relevant repository under github.com/asukatechnologies.
